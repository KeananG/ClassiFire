Wildfires Management Complexity Project

Wildfires from 2014 through 2023

This heat map shows wildfires from 2014 through 2023 in the contiguous USA. The colors are weighted by fire complexity levels ranging from type 1 incidents to type 5 incidents.

Type 1 incidents have a higher weight, but the majority of the red spots indicate large clusters of type 4 and type 5 incidents rather than type 2 or 1 incidents.

This map displays roughly 18,000 fires out of the total 250,000 fires.
